ABOUT THE PROJECT

EverydayGossip was initially developed over 3 weeks to be realeased in December 2014. After publishing the website on the domain everydaygossip.org along with twelve other articles, I applied for Google AdSense and was accepted within a few weeks.

The goal of this website was to provide interesting content to my audience through posting links onto my social media accounts. With the adverts from Google AdSense, I was able to monetise the content I posted to EverydayGossip and by using my social media accounts I was able to generate a lot of advertising revenue.

As the audience of the site increased, this meant that the site required a better performing server to cope with the traffic. Fortunately, thanks to the monetisation of the content this was affordable.


ROLE

My role in the team included creating the final boss battle at the end of the game which invloved editing assets in photoshop, designing the game mechanics and adding the level to the project once everyone finished their tasks.
