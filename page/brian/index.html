ABOUT THE PROJECT

Global Game Jam is an event which takes place over 48 hours. The structure of a jam is usually that everyone gathers on Friday late afternoon, watches a short video keynote with advice from leading game developers, and then a secret theme is announced. All sites worldwide are then challenged to make games based on that same theme, with games to be completed by Sunday afternoon. In January 2015, over 500 locations in 78 countries created 5439 games over the 48 hours!

What do we do now? When zombies take over Brian, a once powerful wizard, is turned into a zombie however retaining most of his mind and powers he pointlessly walks the earth and battling foes to find a point to his now drastically changed life.
