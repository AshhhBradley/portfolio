ABOUT THE PROJECT

Bear With It is a puzzle/platformer game that was developed in Unreal Engine 4.10 with a 3rd person camera view. It combines the abilities of the three main characters to engage the player to overcome physics and logic puzzles in the level that will have multiple methods to complete for achieving each levelâ€™s objective which will be to escape captivity from an unknown entity that has captured all three protagonists.

You play as three main characters; ZomBear, ZomBunny and Elle, but will only be able to control one of them at a time, a group of lovable zombies that are constantly being kidnapped for experimentation.


PROJECT GOALS

Design a non violent game based on the concept of cooperation.
Demonstrate skills in environment puzzle design, and level pacing.
Create a level with simple and fun puzzles to entertain all audiences.


ROLE

My role in the team included creating and planning the initial game concept, creating blueprints for each of the characters attributes, as well as environment design and puzzle design.
